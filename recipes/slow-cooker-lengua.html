Slow Cooker Lengua

Description

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 beef tongue
  • 2 cloves garlic, or more to taste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • salt and ground black pepper to tast
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Instructions

  1. Place beef tongue, onion, garlic, and bay leaf in a slow cooker; generously season with salt. Pour in enough water to cover beef mixture.
  2. Cook on Low for 8 hours.
  3. Transfer beef tongue to a work surface and cool slightly. Peel outer layer of skin from beef tongue and remove rough end.
  4. Chop meat into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chopped meat until tender, 5 to 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
